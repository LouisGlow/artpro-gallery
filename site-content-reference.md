## About / History
From its humble beginnings as Emmanuel Art, ArtPro Gallery has grown.

ArtPro Gallery boasts about four stunning galleries located at Sunninghill Village, Maggies Home of the Chicken Pie, Prison Break Market and Lifestyle Garden Centre, featuring a wide spectrum of South African artists, as well as a range of interior decorative design.

The gallery offers traditional South African artists' unmissable masterpieces, decorative art, investment art, modern carpets, lamps, statues, and services such as framework, commission work, validation certificates, interior design support, auctions and so much more.

ArtPro offers a range of high-end masterpieces, decorative art, modern carpets, statues and decorative lamps. We offer additional services, such as framework, commission work, validation certificates and interior design support at this fine establishment.

Feel free to browse through our selection of artworks. Please reach out to us and let us know how ArtPro Gallery can assist you in your next art selection.
