The furniture shop

Furniture, statues, carpets and lamps - right next to our Maggies Farm gallery in Lanseria.

Next door to Maggies Farm

Right next to our Maggies Farm gallery in Lanseria, the ArtPro furniture shop brings statement furniture and decor together with a curated collection of statues, carpets and lamps.

From bronze and steel sculpture to fine Persian and Oriental carpets and contemporary lamps, it is everything you need to finish a room - and our team is on hand to help you choose.

  • Furniture & decor for every room
  • Bronze, aluminium & steel statues
  • Persian & Oriental carpets - cleaning & repairs
  • Turkish mosaic, resin & carved lamps
