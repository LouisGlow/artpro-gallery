Delivery & shipping

Your piece, transported and delivered with care.

Getting it there

Once you’ve chosen a piece, ArtPro Gallery handles getting it to you. We know how much care goes into selecting art — so whether it’s small, large, valuable or fragile, we transport it safely.

Our trained personnel and proper equipment package and move your artwork securely to your doorstep. Delivery is quoted on distance from the gallery, and international shipping is available on request.

  • Trained handlers & equipment
  • Nationwide, quoted by distance
  • International on request
