From a humble start as Emmanuel Art — a gallery grown across the city.

ArtPro Gallery presents a wide spectrum of South African artists alongside a considered range of interior decorative design. Traditional masterpieces and investment art sit next to modern carpets, statues and decorative lamps.

Beyond the walls, we offer framing, commission work, validation certificates, interior-design support and auctions — the full support an art collection needs, from the first piece to the fiftieth.
