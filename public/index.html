From a humble start as Emmanuel Art — a gallery grown across the city.

ArtPro Gallery presents a wide spectrum of South African artists alongside a considered range of interior decorative design. Traditional masterpieces and investment art sit next to modern carpets, statues and decorative lamps.

Beyond the walls, we offer framing, commission work, validation certificates, interior-design support and auctions — the full support an art collection needs, from the first piece to the fiftieth.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Where are ArtPro Gallery's galleries located?

ArtPro Gallery has four galleries in and around Johannesburg: Sunninghill Village (Sandton, our head office), Prison Break Market (Kyalami), ArtPro @ Lifestyle (Randpark Ridge) and Maggies Farm (Lanseria).

Does ArtPro Gallery deliver artwork?

Yes. We deliver nationwide across South Africa with care, and international shipping is available on request.

Can I commission a custom artwork?

Yes. We arrange bespoke commission work with our represented artists — get in touch with the piece, size and style you have in mind and we'll guide you.

Does ArtPro Gallery offer framing and valuations?

Yes. Alongside the gallery we offer custom framing and professional hanging, art valuations, and validation certificates of authenticity.

How many artists does ArtPro Gallery represent?

We represent a growing roster of more than 50 South African artists, from traditional masters to contemporary voices, across a wide range of mediums.

Can I view the art online?

Yes. Browse works for sale by medium in the shop, explore the collection by artist, or step into our interactive virtual gallery from any device.
