A gallery grown across the city

From a humble start as Emmanuel Art to four galleries and fifty represented artists.

ArtPro Gallery boasts four stunning galleries - at Sunninghill Village, Maggies Home of the Chicken Pie, Prison Break Market and Lifestyle Garden Centre - featuring a wide spectrum of South African artists alongside a range of interior decorative design.

The gallery offers traditional South African masterpieces, decorative art and investment art, together with modern carpets, lamps and statues. Around the artwork we provide the services a collection needs: framing, commission work, validation certificates, interior-design support, auctions and more.

Feel free to browse our selection of artworks. Reach out and let us know how ArtPro Gallery can assist you in your next art selection.
